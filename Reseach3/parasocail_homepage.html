What is a parasocial relationship?
_____________________________

One-sided relationships, where one person extends emotional energy, interest and time,
and the other party, the persona, is completely unaware of the other's existence.

Parasocial relationships are most common with celebrities, organizations
(such as sports teams) or television stars.
